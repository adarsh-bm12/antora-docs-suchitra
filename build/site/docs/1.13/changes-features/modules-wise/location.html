R000

Location (applicable to Nymbl Suite ERP-v2.25.37.75)

On the Location page, a confirmation popup containing a Yes or No button, and the message "Is the change of Device/Journey desired? Catalog settings will be affected." will be displayed when the Journey/Single KDS toggle button is attempted to be changed by the user.

On confirming this, it will affect the catalog product group (KDS Journey or KDS Device fields) settings.
